About JIMS

One of the Top GGSIPU Affiliated Colleges in Delhi NCR

JIMS Engineering Management Technical Campus is one of the top GGSIPU affiliated colleges in the Delhi/NCR that provides steadfast learning spaces within smart classrooms, well-equipped workshops, and extensive learning zones, a well established institute with some of the latest technologies.

The entire campus is Wi-Fi enabled with high speed internet. Spacious air-conditioned classrooms with projectors and internet connectivity make digital learning easy. The campus is CCTV enabled with 24x7 security, and features a well-stocked library covering all subjects.
